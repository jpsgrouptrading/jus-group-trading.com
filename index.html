About Us

JPS Group Trading is a commodity trading firm based in Australia with a subsidiary in Singapore, specializing exclusively in physical commodity trading across key global markets.


We focus on the physical supply, distribution and logistics of energy, metals, agricultural products and bulk raw materials, leveraging our strategic locations and global network to connect producers and buyers worldwide.


With deep industry expertise, stable supply channels and rigorous risk control, we have built long-term strategic partnerships with global producers, logistics providers and industrial clients, establishing a leading reputation in physical commodity trading.

LEONAUS GROUP

JPS GROUP TRADING PTY LTD and JPS GROUP TRADE PTE LTD are both trading subsidiaries operating under the LEONAUS Group umbrella.

Transportation Infrastructure

With over 18 years of extensive experience in the rail and transit sector, we have been deeply involved in several of Australia’s most iconic infrastructure projects, including the Sydney Metro (MTMS) and the Sydney CBD & South East Light Rail. We specialize in enhancing the safety, reliability, and operational efficiency of rail and light rail public transport systems.

Real Estate Investment & Development

In the real estate sector, we provide customized land development, luxury residences, and high-yield investment properties. By engaging in early-stage land development, we drive value appreciation through precise planning approvals and expert project management, while also undertaking comprehensive construction projects across the residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Core Commodities

Refined Oil Products

We specialize in the international trade of various refined petroleum products, including but not limited to Gasoline, Diesel, Jet Fuel (Aviation Kerosene), and Fuel Oil. Through close partnerships with global refineries and end-users, we leverage deep market insights to provide our clients with highly competitive sourcing and sales opportunities.

Crude Oil

As a significant player in the global crude oil market, we supply a diverse range of crude grades from major producing regions worldwide. We possess a profound understanding of market complexities and volatility. Our mission is to serve as a reliable bridge between producers and consumers, offering flexible and strategic trading solutions.

Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG)

In response to the evolving global energy landscape, we have intensified our focus on the LNG sector. We excel in supply-demand analysis and logistics optimization, ensuring that our clients receive secure, reliable, and cost-effective LNG trading solutions.

Copper

As a critical industrial metal, copper remains in high demand across the power, construction, and electronics sectors. We focus on the international trade of Copper Concentrate and Copper Cathodes. Through expert market assessment and robust supply chain management, we ensure the delivery of high-quality copper products to meet our clients' production and investment needs.
